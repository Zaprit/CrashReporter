Having issues with Beacon?

This form allows you to report issues you're encountering with LBP Union's Beacon, our official Lighthouse instance.

Before reporting, ensure you can reproduce the issue. This is important as we will most likely attempt to reproduce the issue using the steps you have provided.

Check our status page to ensure we have not already logged an incident and/or a planned maintenance window.

Do not enter any personally identifiable information, your Beacon password, or any other form of credentials.

Upon submitting, do not share your Report UUID to anyone. Staff members will automatically receive your report details, which includes your Report UUID.
